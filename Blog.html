Welcome to Ha Giang

Welcome to the Horse Travel Hostel Ha Giang Loop Tours blog page, where you will find all the information you need before embarking on your Ha Giang Loop adventure.

The Ha Giang Loop is a breathtaking circular route located in the far North of Vietnam, near the
China border. Renowned as one of the most stunning regions in the country, it spans
approximately 350 to 400km. The journey begins in Ha Giang city and takes around 3-4 days (or
longer depending on your schedule) on a motorbike to complete, allowing you to immerse yourself
in the captivating beauty of the area and stay overnight in various charming villages along the way.
